Privacy Policy

This section explains what data we handle and why. A short, more detailed technical description also lives in our internal data-flow documentation.

What we collect

  • Account data — your email, company, and role.
  • Error data you submit — the extracted text of error messages, the screen name, an optional version hint, and any context you type. Screenshots are processed to read the error text but are not stored by us.
  • Usage & resolution records — which solution was shown, whether it was marked helpful, and (for paid resolutions) the amount and payment reference.
  • Billing data — handled by Stripe; we store only your customer/subscription status, not card numbers.

How error data is protected

Your submitted error text and resolution records are private to your company and enforced at the database level — other companies cannot read them. Screenshots are transient: they are sent for text extraction and not retained.

Service providers we use

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and hosting of your account and error data.
  • Vercel — hosts the server functions that broker AI and billing requests (it does not persist your requests).
  • Anthropic — reads the error text from screenshots and helps assemble guidance. Screenshots and text pass through its API transiently for processing; per Anthropic's terms, API inputs are not used to train its models by default.
  • Stripe — payment processing.
  • [Email/SMTP provider] — sends account emails (sign-up confirmation, etc.).

Retention

Account and error/resolution records are retained while your account is active, and your company can clear its error archive at any time. Screenshots are not retained. Billing records are retained as required for accounting and tax.

Your choices

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting admin@ifsguru.com. Deleting your account removes your associated personal records, subject to records we must keep for legal or accounting reasons.

International users

If you access the Service from outside the operator's country, your data may be processed in other regions by the providers above. A full data-protection statement (including GDPR-specific rights and legal bases) will be provided before any marketing to international users.

Cookies & local storage
